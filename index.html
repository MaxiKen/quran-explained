About

A modern, interactive Quran reader designed to help you explore and understand the Noble Quran with phrase-by-phrase explanations. Click on any English phrase to read its detailed commentary.

Features
  • 📖
    All 114 ChaptersComplete chapter listing with Arabic names, meanings, and verse counts
  • 🔍
    Smart SearchSearch chapters by name, number, meaning, or search within verse content (English & Arabic)
  • 💬
    Interactive ExplanationsClickable phrase-by-phrase English translation with detailed tafsir commentary
  • 🔖
    Verse BookmarksBookmark specific verses for quick access later — saved locally on your device
  • 🕐
    Reading HistoryTrack which verse explanations you've read — never lose your place
  • 🎨
    4 Color ThemesDark, Light, Sepia, and Midnight modes for comfortable reading any time
Offline Access

This app works offline

Once you've visited a chapter, it's cached on your device and available without an internet connection.

  • 1
    Install the app Tap Add to Home Screen (iOS Safari) or Install App (Android / Chrome) from your browser menu
  • 2
    Browse chapters while online Open any chapter — it will be silently cached on your device for offline reading
  • 3
    Read anytime, anywhere Go offline and open the app — all previously visited chapters remain fully accessible
